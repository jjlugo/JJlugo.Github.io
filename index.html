The Gap in Education

My poster tackles the educational gap by race. I believe that in a nation where we aim for equality, we should all be exposed to the best education the nation has to offer. I created the word “GAP” with pencils and then photographed it. I separated the word to create a conceptual gap. And then, I generated information that is relevant to the cause.

End Domestic Violence

This poster is a typographical portrayal of domestic violence. It portrays how violence should end, it encourages those who are victims to take action. I decided to emphasize "End Violence" as a design choice to let the type speak for itself.

Domestic Violence

Family also plays a huge part in cases of domestic violence. This posters displays a brother embracing his sister. The background is blurred out to make the siblings more prominent and focused in the poster.

Domestic Violence in same-sex

Domestic violence is also seeing in same sex couples. This poster displays two girls, one that's bruised and her abuser. The victim still seeks the company of her abuser. In this poster i tried to show the victim's point of view as a way to create awareness that is not easy for people to seek help.
